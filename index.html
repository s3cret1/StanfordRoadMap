Pro Tip

Mindset Check

Small, steady progress beats last-minute heroics. Rest is a strategic part of application success.

Jan to Mar (Jr): Apply to College Prep Scholars (CPS) & Young Leaders Summit (YLS)
Mar to Oct (Jr): Start SAT/ACT prep. Aim for a 1370+
Ap to Aug (Jr): Apply to summer fly-ins to explore universities. Draft essays (personal statement) in the summer & research your top universities :)
