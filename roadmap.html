Roadmap: Junior → Senior (month-by-month)

QuestBridge deadlines change yearly so please verify official dates beforehand

Junior Year (Big picture)

Goal: strengthen ECs, join CPS & YLS, and begin writing & research early

Jan to Mar

Apply to College Prep Scholars (CPS)

CPS is a great honor to list on college apps (25% acceptance rate), and if accepted, you show that you are already competitve for admissions

  • Complete CPS essays & activities (Almost 1:1 to QB's NCM application)
  • Gather transcripts, counselor info (+ school profile), and one recommender
  • Gain access to summer programs and essay prep resources
Feb to Apr

Plan senior-year schedule & mentors

Avoid avoidable gaps: many colleges expect 3+ years of science or equivalent rigor.

  • Create a 4-year plan with core classes (DE courses)
  • Apply to Matriculate or another free advising program
  • Ask teachers early to be recommenders
Mar to Oct

SAT: start serious prep

Many universities (Stanford) are requiring test scores. Use official practice material and timed tests.

  • Take practice BlueBook tests
  • Study the SAT question bank questions
  • Schedule at least two test dates (aim for 1350+), and request a fee waiver from Mrs. Contreras
Apr to Aug

Fly-ins & summer

Fly-ins are free, so apply to the ones that interest you (or everything :D )

  • Apply broadly to fall fly-ins (RSI, SSP, MITES, SIMR)
  • Summer programs SHOULD be free
  • Draft your essays while fresh in summer
Jun to Nov

Essays & research

Start supplements early. Rank only colleges you’d be excited to attend.

  • Draft personal statement & supplement drafts
  • Get honest feedback (especially from AI or your matriculate rating your essays) & revise multiple times
  • Do a lot of research! Use the A2C and QuestBridge subreddits
Summer → Senior

Senior-year prep

Finalize last SAT scores (last to retake is October), secure recommenders, and prepare documents for the QB Finalist requirements if needed.

  • Confirm test sending & dates
  • Fill out the FAFSA/CSS ASAP
  • Look at the past college supplemental prompts to get a head start :)

Senior year & Match-specific timeline

Common QuestBridge timeline items (double-check official site each year):

  • QB app was due on Sept 30, & Match Rankings Form opens a day later. Finalist status was released on Oct. 21 (confirm dates on QuestBridge site)
  • If a finalist: be prepared to do a LOT of supplementals in 2 weeks
  • If matched: you must follow the partner college's admitted-student steps & congratzz
Common Mistake

Ranking schools you wouldn’t attend just to increase chances. The Match is binding so please only rank schools you’d happily join.

Daily & weekly habits that work

  • Daily: 25–40 minutes of focused prep (tests or essays) + one short recharge activity.
  • Weekly: at least one full-length practice test or a polished essay revision.
  • Monthly: review timelines, update your templates, and check in with an advisor.
Pro Tip

Write early and revise until it stings. Essays are where voice and reflection win.
